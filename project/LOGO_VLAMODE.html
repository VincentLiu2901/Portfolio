VLAMODE Logo Design

VLAMODE comes from the French word for “fashion” and is a brand identity project built around elegance and modern style. The logo combines the initials V and L from my name, Vincent Liu. The V represents the heel of a high-heeled shoe, while the L forms the body. Together, they create a clean and symbolic design that connects my personal identity with the fashion world.

Logo Details & Guidelines

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Design Process: The logo idea started by exploring how my personal identity could connect with fashion. Through many sketches and digital trials, the letters V and L were shaped into the outline of a high-heeled shoe. This design feels modern and elegant, while also giving the logo both personal meaning and a clear link to the fashion industry.

Do's:

  • Ensure high contrast for maximum readability
  • Always use the approved brand colors
  • Maintain copyright and trademark compliance when using the logo commercially
  • Keep adequate clear space around the logo

Don'ts:

  • Do not alter the shape, colors, or proportions
  • Do not add shadows, gradients, or effects unless specified in the identity system
  • Do not use the logo without authorization
  • Do not place the logo on backgrounds that compromise readability

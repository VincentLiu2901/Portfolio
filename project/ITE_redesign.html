Intelligent Tech Engine (ITE) Strategic Design Plan

The ITE Strategic Design Plan was created as part of an advanced design course project that challenged our team to connect design research, brand strategy, and creative communication into one unified document. ITE is envisioned as a technology integration platform for small- and medium-sized e-commerce businesses struggling with digital tool fragmentation.

Many businesses today are overwhelmed by the growing number of digital tools—AI services, automation platforms, data dashboards, and APIs. While each tool is powerful, they often fail to integrate seamlessly. Our mission was to position ITE as the bridge between people and technology—a brand that simplifies complexity, builds trust, and makes technology feel human rather than intimidating.

Project Overview

Course: Advanced Visual Design (DSGN–304–B)

Project Type: UX/Brand Strategy Documentation (Team Project)

Team Members: Alex Armstrong, Wenxi (Vincent) Liu, Thi Anh Dao (Hana) Nguyen, Marius Tanglao

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Research & Strategy Documentation

Design Goals: Connect design research, brand strategy, and creative communication into one unified framework. Position ITE as a technology integration platform that simplifies complexity, builds trust, and makes technology feel human rather than intimidating for e-commerce businesses.

Research & Key Insights:

Problem Space:

  • Businesses struggle to connect different digital services together
  • Many platforms focus on features over usability, causing tool fatigue
  • Trust in AI and automation is fragile—companies want clarity and accountability

Key Insights:

  • Users feel exhausted by tools that don't integrate well
  • Adoption increases when tech feels human and supportive, not robotic
  • Strong brand identity must project warmth and reliability, not just innovation

Brand Strategy:

Vision: To help e-commerce businesses adopt technology that thinks like humans do, creating systems that are clear, connected, and trustworthy.

Mission: To serve as the bridge between innovation and usability, making powerful technology accessible without overwhelming users.

Design Principles:

  • Simplicity: Reduce cognitive load with clear communication and intuitive systems
  • System Thinking: Treat every touchpoint as part of a larger, connected ecosystem
  • Trust-Building: Prioritize reliability, transparency, and consistency
  • Human Warmth: Infuse the brand with empathy, personality, and positive tone

12-Month Strategic Roadmap:

  • Phase 1 (0–4 months): Brand identity, messaging, visual guidelines
  • Phase 2 (4–8 months): Service rollout, onboarding flow, first client acquisition
  • Phase 3 (8–12 months): Growth scaling, user trust metrics, refinement cycles

Creative Campaign: AI & Tech Confessions

We proposed a storytelling-driven marketing campaign where AI and tech "confess their failures" in a humorous, relatable way. This narrative highlights common frustrations, makes technology approachable, and positions ITE as the solution that restores control and confidence.

Key Performance Indicators:

  • User Adoption Rate (onboarding success)
  • Integration Efficiency (time saved by businesses)
  • Trust Metrics (survey-based confidence ratings)
  • Customer Retention

Project Outcome:

The ITE Strategic Design Plan successfully transformed a vague idea into a detailed, actionable, and creative brand framework. It provided a roadmap not only for building ITE's visual identity but also for ensuring long-term growth and user trust, showing how design strategy goes beyond visuals to create practical and inspiring solutions.

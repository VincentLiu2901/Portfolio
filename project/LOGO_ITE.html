Intelligent Tech Engine (ITE) Logo & Brand Identity

Intelligent Tech Engine (ITE) was founded in 2025 to help small and medium businesses integrate modern technologies like AI, automation, and data platforms into their operations. Many companies struggle to connect these tools efficiently, causing delays and inefficiencies. ITE acts as a smart bridge, providing a flexible and easy-to-integrate tech engine that helps businesses work smarter and faster.

The logo design reflects ITE's mission: it combines the letters i, t, and e in a bold, square-shaped layout that is both recognizable and versatile for digital applications, including app icons. The lowercase "e" cleverly uses negative space to hint at the "t" and "i", adding subtle layers of meaning while maintaining a clean and modern appearance.

Brand Identity & Guidelines

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Figma

Design Goals: Create a professional, tech-oriented identity for an AI and automation company. Design a versatile logo for digital, print, and corporate use. Reflect clarity, intelligence, and innovation through visual design while ensuring brand materials are clean, consistent, and accessible.

Professional Color Palette:

Primary Blue
#5754FF
Secondary Dark Blue
#2E3192
Text Black
#000000
Background White
#FFFFFF

Typography: Myriad Variable Concept, with flexible sizing for optimal readability across all applications.

Do's:

  • Use official brand colors and maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use high-quality, sharp logo images
  • Apply full logo for formal use, icon-only for small or digital spaces
  • Place on simple backgrounds for maximum visibility
  • Maintain minimum size of 60px height

Don'ts:

  • Don't stretch, rotate, or change the logo proportions
  • Don't use unapproved colors, gradients, or effects
  • Don't alter the font or rearrange letters
  • Don't place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't use the logo below minimum size requirements

Brand Voice & Tone:

Clear, confident, and solution-driven. Direct and modern, approachable but professional. Uses concise, precise language while avoiding unnecessary jargon or casual phrases.

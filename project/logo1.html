LAMODE Logo Design

LAMODE, derived from the French word for fashion, is a brand identity project focused on elegance and modernity. The logo was created by combining the initials V and L, representing my name: Vincent Liu. The design draws inspiration from the form of a high-heeled shoe: the V symbolizes the heel, while the L represents the body of the shoe. Together, they form a sleek and symbolic mark that reflects both personal identity and the world of fashion.

About Me

My name is Vincent Liu, a second year User Experience Design student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology. I am passionate about improving digital experiences by addressing common usability challenges that are often overlooked. I enjoy observing human behavior and exploring the psychology behind user interactions, which helps me design with empathy and purpose. Outside of design, I pursue drawing as a creative outlet. My goal is to craft designs that are simple, unique, and meaningful.

User Research

Observing people, asking questions, and finding user needs.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Turning ideas into sketches and clickable designs.

Visual Design

Using drawing and creativity to make clean, appealing layouts.

Usability Testing

Checking if designs are easy for people to use.

Critical Thinking

Analyzing problems and making thoughtful design choices.

Problem-Solving

Finding simple solutions to fix frustrating user experiences.
